About Us

Kumi Association is a bridge connecting suppliers of raw or semi-finished products with customers seeking value-added solutions. We enhance products through thoughtful design, customization, and exceptional service, ensuring every interaction uplifts and empowers.

The name "Kumi" draws dual inspiration: from the Aramaic "Koumi" in the biblical phrase "Talitha Koumi" (Mark 5:41), meaning "arise" or "be uplifted," symbolizing revival and empowerment. It also resonates with the Japanese "Kumi" (ÁµÑ), which signifies "group," "team," or "assembly"‚Äîreflecting our commitment to fostering collaborative associations. In other Japanese contexts, it evokes "beauty" and "long-cherished" qualities, mirroring our pursuit of enduring, harmonious partnerships.

Our commitment is rooted in faith and excellence, working with all our heart as unto the Lord.
